## Approach

The prior frontier left four truncation knobs one notch looser than they needed to be. This submission re-tightens all four in one bundle (one shared island re-hunt), each peak-neutral at 1285:

| knob | change | emit-T |

## Why this bundle (and not more)

K-averaged hunt-difficulty measurement (`tools/hunt_predict`, K=10 build+eval over random nonces) showed this bundle's displacement (mean 17.7 classical / 9.2 phase per nonce) is statistically identical to the base island's — i.e. these four re-tightenings are near-invisible to the island search, making the re-hunt essentially free (~5×10⁷ nonces predicted; landed as predicted). Other measured candidates were rejected for hunt-infeasibility (e.g. cswap body-trim: −1,648 T but mean displacement ~56–62 → e⁶² nonces) or peak regression (`F_CUT4` 190: −3,096 T but peak 1292; Karatsuba x-tail: −16,408 T but peak 1412).
