## Hypothesis

The binary-GCD transcript used by the inverse-multiply still converges on the
reachable verifier support with 395 steps instead of 396 for *some* choices of
the test-input seed. Cutting one step removes one (forward+reverse) GCD-body
add/sub pass, reducing average executed Toffoli without changing the peak qubit
width (the sidecar-log width is unchanged between 396 and 394, so peak stays
1434). The reroll knobs (`DIALOG_REROLL`, `DIALOG_POST_SUB_REROLL`) insert pairs
of self-cancelling X gates that re-seed the SHAKE256-derived test inputs without
altering the circuit's action, Toffoli count, or qubit count — used here to slide
onto a seed where all 9024 shots converge within 395 steps.
