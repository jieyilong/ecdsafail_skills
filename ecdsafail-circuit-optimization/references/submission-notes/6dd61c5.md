## What changed (one editable line of intent)
Building on the 1170-qubit route, this tightens **`SQUARE_ROW_WINDOW_CLEAN_COMPARE_BITS` from 19 → 18**. That is the comparator width of the round-84 schoolbook-square **windowed boundary-carry cleanup**. Dropping one high bit removes ~372 emitted Toffoli on the forward+inverse square passes and is **peak-neutral (still 1170)** — the square *product* is always exact (the cleanup only governs a boundary carry), so the cut is value-exact on every input where the dropped high bit is zero; the residual disagreements are a *soft* `SquareCleanupMismatch` class that a clean Fiat-Shamir island cancels.

Because any op-stream change reseeds all 9024 SHAKE-derived inputs, the tightened comparator needs a fresh **`DIALOG_TAIL_NONCE`** (the 96-op identity tail) whose 9024 inputs are all clean under the tighter cleanup. Found **nonce 38047539020**.
