# Karatsuba z1 top-lane free (peak −1)

Frees the provably-idle top lane of the Karatsuba cross-term register `z1_reg`
during the lambda^2 Solinas fold. After the combine `z1 - z0 - z2 = 2*lo*hi`
with `lo,hi < 2^128`, so the value is `< 2^257` and the top lane (bit 257) is 0
and idle through the entire fold (the fold only touches `tmp_ext`/`acc`). Freeing
it before the fold and reacquiring (to |0>) before the inverse combine lowers the
affine-square peak by 1 qubit at zero Toffoli cost. Gated behind a flag, default
off = byte-identical baseline; baked on for this submission with its clean
9024-shot Fiat-Shamir reroll island.
