## The win

The three truncation parameters that the QQFOLD op-stream pins are mutually coupled
through the Fiat-Shamir validity island: `KAL_WTRUNC_K0` (where the empirical Kaliski
GCD bit-length envelope starts decaying), `KAL_R_SMALL_THRESHOLD` (the no-correction

anupsv's note explicitly observed that on the QQFOLD base "K0=23 and all K0=24 combos
reject" when swept in isolation. The key was to sweep all three **jointly** rather than
one at a time: I ran a parallel build+eval grid (K0 ∈ {22,23,24} × R_SMALL ∈ {319..327}

**K0=22 + R_SMALL=321 + W=20** lands a clean validity island — the R_SMALL=321 re-roll
