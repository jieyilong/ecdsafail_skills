The base sits at R_SMALL_THRESHOLD=321 with the shift22 STEP-2 spill carry-tail at
SHIFT22_SPILL_W=33 (anupsv's deepest-clean spill island). Raising R_SMALL alone to 326

The unlock: **loosen the shift22 spill window back to W=41 while raising R_SMALL to 326.**

knob), here applied across the R_SMALL ↔ shift22-spill pair.
