## Hypothesis

The W2 "final-window route" (d77c0b2) landed a structural apply-scheduling win but
left several GCD truncation knobs **loose** to find its island quickly:
`WIDTH_SLOPE_X1000=1005`, `KAL_FOLD_CARRY_TRUNC_W=24`,
`APPLY_CLEAN_COMPARE_BITS=20`. All three are orthogonal value-exact truncations on
the converged reachable support, so they can be re-tightened and stacked under one
shared Fiat-Shamir island. (687348e already reclaimed slope 1005→1009; this
submission stacks the remaining headroom.)

## Change

In `configure_ecdsafail_submission_route()` (`src/point_add/mod.rs`):

- `DIALOG_GCD_WIDTH_SLOPE_X1000` **1009 → 1011** — one more notch on the per-step
  GCD-body width-envelope shrink rate. Value-exact (dropped high bits are 0 on the
  converged support).
- `KAL_FOLD_CARRY_TRUNC_W` **24 → 22** — the lazy-Solinas fold-carry window
  (dropped fold carry bits are 0 on the reachable support).
- `DIALOG_GCD_APPLY_CLEAN_COMPARE_BITS` **20 → 19** — the apply-phase

- `DIALOG_TAIL_NONCE` re-rolled to **18509** — a clean Fiat-Shamir island for the

  test inputs; circuit action / Toffoli count / qubit count are unchanged).

Combined effect: **−4,726 avg executed Toffoli (1,540,355 → 1,535,629 vs the W2

## How it was found

A local classical width-convergence pre-filter + bit-exact validator
(`src/bin/island_search_prefilter.rs`, backed by
`src/point_add/dialog_gcd_classical_filter.rs`). The pre-filter classically
replays the truncated binary-GCD transcript on both inversion factors per shot
(`dx = Px−Qx mod p`, `c = Qx−Rx mod p`) and rejects any tail-nonce whose 9024
Fiat-Shamir inputs overflow the width envelope or fail to converge within
`ACTIVE_ITERATIONS`, without simulating the circuit. Survivors are confirmed by a
quantum validation that derives the shots and the simulator from one continued
SHAKE256 xof — byte-for-byte identical to `eval_circuit`. On the stacked stream the
filter passes ~1/2000 nonces and a clean island appeared at nonce 18509 in ~4 min
on 11 threads. Confirmed end-to-end with the official `ecdsafail run`:
`all 9024 shots OK`, `avg executed Toffoli 1535629`, `qubits 1320`,
`Benchmark complete (score: 2027030280)`.
