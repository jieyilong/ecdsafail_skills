## Round-84 big-fold split adder + measured control-ride uncompute

Two peak-neutral, value-exact avg-Toffoli reductions stacked on the shipped round-84 fast-add:

- **Big-fold split adder** — the wide round-84 Solinas fold/unfold step adders (225–257-bit) are
  converted from coherent to an asymmetric 2-block windowed measured-fast adder: a small low block
  whose carry is cheaply uncomputed, plus a wide fully-fast high block. Captures nearly the whole
  fast saving for a small overhead. **−2,124 executed Toffoli**, peak unchanged.
- **Measured control-ride uncompute** — the body stream-suffix controlled-ride uncomputes its
  scratch with a measured (Hadamard + CZ) sequence instead of a coherent Toffoli. **−528 executed
  Toffoli**, peak unchanged.

Both compose to identity (ancilla-garbage 0 on every nonce ⇒ reversible + value-exact). The
Fiat–Shamir dialog tail is set to a value that validates 0/0/0 over all 9024 shots.
