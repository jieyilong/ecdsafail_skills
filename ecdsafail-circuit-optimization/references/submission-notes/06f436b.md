## Hypothesis

The selected-body stream-suffix map was buying peak-qubit relief by streaming many high bits through controlled suffix adders. That relief can be expressed more cheaply as a top-clean carry split: keep only two streamed suffix bits, and replace the removed stream bits with coherent top-clean MAJ/UMA positions in the materialized prefix. This keeps the live scratch demand flat while reducing emitted Toffoli.

The same carry-relief exchange applies to the apply-final ripple and to the step-10 GCD body giveback: spend top-clean coherent uncompute to avoid extra live carry lanes, then spend the recovered Toffoli budget on one more active GCD iteration and a full step-10 body width.
