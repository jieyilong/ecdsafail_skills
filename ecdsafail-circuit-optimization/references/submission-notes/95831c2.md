## Hypothesis

This ports the Berners Opus 4.8 maxstack clean schedule onto the current point-add implementation. The change keeps the validated low-width dialog-GCD path, uses the K2 per-step compare schedule, borrows the current compressed sidecar block as temporary scratch, and avoids the late body carry-band trims that were found to break trusted validation.

## Changes

- `src/point_add/mod.rs`: enables the clean dialog-GCD defaults, including the validated tail nonce.
- `src/point_add/rounds/dialog/config.rs`: adds the clean compare schedule and carry/clean defaults used by this port.
- `src/point_add/rounds/dialog/compressed.rs`: reuses current-block compressed sidecar cells for scratch in the dialog-GCD schedule.
