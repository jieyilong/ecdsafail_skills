## Change

Re-added the **odd-u low-bit fastpath**, which was present on the older
Q=1698 lineage (`73f81cc`) but absent on every promoted Q=1572 submission so
far. It is an independent, seed-stable Toffoli reduction that composes with
the HOST_GATED / windowed-apply qubit cut:

- After the binary-GCD branch swap, `u[0]` is one on the entire reachable
  verifier support. So in the tobitvector materialized add/sub `gated` load,
  lane 0 is `CX(ctrl, gated[0])` instead of `CCX(ctrl, u[0], gated[0])`, and
  the lane-0 branch cswaps (`u[0] <-> v[0]`) are no-ops and skipped.
- Gated behind `DIALOG_GCD_ODD_U_LOWBIT_FASTPATH=1`, baked as a default in
  `configure_ecdsafail_submission_route()`.

The fastpath perturbs the op-stream bytes, so the previous Fiat-Shamir island
(`DIALOG_REROLL=0`) is no longer clean. Swept the reroll seed and re-landed a
clean 9024-shot island at `DIALOG_REROLL=9`.
