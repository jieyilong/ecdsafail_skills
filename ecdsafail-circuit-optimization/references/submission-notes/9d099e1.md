## Idea
After the round84 affine-square peak was driven down to the apply tier (1543), I profiled the
per-phase active-qubit maxima and found the round84 Solinas **doubling** lanes
(`r84k_sol_dbl22` / `r84k_sol_halve`) sit at peak **1538 — 5 qubits below the binder (1543)**.
That slack is free room: the doubling lanes default to the low-peak (ancilla-free, higher-Toffoli)
`mod_double_inplace_direct_const_fast` variant precisely *because round84 used to bind*. It no
longer binds, so switching those lanes to the fast carry-ancilla variant is free at peak 1543 and
**value-exact** (identical result on `acc`).
